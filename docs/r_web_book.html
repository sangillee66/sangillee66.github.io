Spatial Data Science

  • Pebesma, Edzer, and Roger Bivand. 2023. Spatial Data Science: With Applications in R. 1st edition. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Lovelace, Robin, Jakub Nowosad, and Jannes Muenchow. 2025. Geocomputation with R. 2nd edition. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Wimberly, Michael C. 2023. Geographic Data Science with R: Visualizing and Analyzing Environmental Change. 1st edition. Boca Raton London New York: Chapman; Hall/CRC.

  • Rey, Sergio, Dani Arribas-Bel, and Levi John Wolf. 2023. Geographic Data Science with Python. 1st edition. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Anselin, Luc, Grant Morrison, Angela Li, and Karina Acosta. 2023. Hands-on Spatial Data Science with r. https://spatialanalysis.github.io/handsonspatialdata/index.html.

  • Sabbata, Stefano De. 2024. R for Geographic Data Science. https://sdesabbata.github.io/r-for-geographic-data-science/.

  • Comber, Lex. 2023. GeoComputation and Spatial Analysis Practicals. https://bookdown.org/lexcomber/GEOG3195/.

  • Davis, Jerry, and SFSU Institute for Geographic Information Science. 2024. Introduction to Environmental Data Science. https://bookdown.org/igisc/EnvDataSci/.

  • Dorman, Michael. 2023. Introduction to Spatial Data Programming with R. https://geobgu.xyz/r/.

  • Spatial Data Science with R. https://rspatial.org/raster/.

  • Spatial Data Science with R and “terra”. https://rspatial.org/.

Spatial Data Analysis and Spatial Statistics

  • Paez, Antonio. 2024. An Introduction to Spatial Data Analysis and Statistics: A Course in R. https://paezha.github.io/spatial-analysis-r/.

  • Moraga, Paula. 2023. Spatial Statistics for Data Science: Theory and Practice with R. 1st edition. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Rowe, Francisco, and Dani Arribas-Bel. 2024. Spatial Modelling for Data Scientists. https://gdsl-ul.github.io/san/.

  • Geomatics for Environmental Management: An Open Textbook for Students and Practitioners.  https://www.opengeomatics.ca/.

  • Arribas-Bel, Francisco Rowe, Dani. 2023. Spatial Modelling for Data Scientists. https://gdsl-ul.github.io/san/.

  • Lovelace, Robin, and Morgane Dumont. 2017. Spatial Microsimulation with R. 1st edition. Chapman; Hall/CRC.

Cartography and GeoVisualization

  • Sievert, Carson. 2020. Interactive Web-Based Data Visualization with R, Plotly, and Shiny. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Dorman, Michael. 2024. Introduction to Web Mapping. https://geobgu.xyz/web-mapping2/index.html.

  • Tennekes, Martijn, and Jakub Nowosad. 2021. Elegant and Informative Maps with Tmap. https://r-tmap.github.io/tmap-book/.

  • Protić, Milan Kilibarda i Dragutin. 2019. Introduction to Geovisualization and Web Cartography. https://osgl.grf.bg.ac.rs/books/gvvk-en/.

  • Fidan, Emine. Guide to Creating Interactive Maps in R. https://bookdown.org/eneminef/DRR_Bookdown/.

  • Dorman, Michael. 2024. Introduction to Web Mapping. https://geobgu.xyz/web-mapping2/index.html.

  • Hahn, Nico. 2020. Making Maps with R. https://bookdown.org/nicohahn/making_maps_with_r5/docs/introduction.html.

Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

  • Slingsby, Jasper. 2024. A Minimal Introduction to GIS (in R). https://www.ecologi.st/spatial-r/.

  • Dorman, Michael. 2024. Introduction to Spatial Data Programming with r. https://geobgu.xyz/r/.

  • Lovelace, Robin, Jakub Nowosad, and Jannes Muenchow. 2025. Geocomputation with R. 2nd edition. Boca Raton: CRC.

  • Gimond, Manuel. 2023. Intro to GIS and Spatial Analysis. https://mgimond.github.io/Spatial/index.html.

  • Engel, Claudia A. 2024. Using Spatial Data with r. https://cengel.github.io/R-spatial/.

  • Mieno, Taro.  R as GIS for Economists. https://tmieno2.github.io/R-as-GIS-for-Economists/index.html.

  • Branion-Calles, Michael. 2021. A Crash Course in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Using R. https://bookdown.org/michael_bcalles/gis-crash-course-in-r/.

  • Geomatics for Environmental Management: An Open Textbook for Students and Practitioners.  https://www.opengeomatics.ca/.

Remote Sensing

  • Camara, Gilberto, Rolf Simoes, Felipe Souza, Felipe Menino, Charlotte Pelletier, Pedro R. Andrade, Karine Ferreira, and Gilberto Queiroz. n.d. Satellite Image Time Series Analysis on Earth Observation Data Cubes. https://e-sensing.github.io/sitsbook/.

Applied Spatial Data Science

  • Walker, Kyle. 2023. Analyzing US Census Data. 1st edition. Boca Raton: Chapman; Hall/CRC.

  • Moraga, Paula. 2019. Geospatial Health Data: Modeling and Visualization with R-INLA and Shiny. CRC Press.

  • Solymosi, Reka, and Juanjo Medina. 2022. Crime Mapping in r. https://maczokni.github.io/crime_mapping_textbook/.

  • Steif, Ken. 2021. Public Policy Analytics: Code and Context for Data Science in Government. 1st edition. Boca Raton London New York: CRC Press.

  • Gannon, J. P. 2024. Hydroinformatics. https://vt-hydroinformatics.github.io/Quarto_Book/.

  • Wagner, Helene, ed. 2024. Landscape Genetic Data Analysis with R. https://bookdown.org/hhwagner1/LandGenCourse_book/.
